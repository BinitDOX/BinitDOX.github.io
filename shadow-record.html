Privacy Policy for ShadowRecord

Effective Date: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ShadowRecord. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. ShadowRecord is designed as an offline-first, privacy-centric audio buffer tool.

2. Information Collection and Use

ShadowRecord does not collect, store, or transmit your audio recordings to any server. All audio recording, processing, and storage happen locally on your device.

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, such as Google AdMob (for advertisements) and Firebase Crashlytics (for anonymous crash reports).

3. Microphone Usage

ShadowRecord requests access to your device's Microphone to buffer audio in memory and save it to storage when triggered. Data is processed locally and never uploaded to the cloud.

4. Internet Usage

The app requires Internet access solely for loading advertisements and sending anonymous crash reports. Core recording functionality works completely offline.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub.
