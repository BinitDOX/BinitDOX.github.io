Privacy Policy

App: FakePowerOff

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Fake Power Off app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is secure.

2. Information Collection and Use

TL;DR: The Fake Power Off application does not collect any personal data from its users.

We do not collect, store, or share any information about you or your device.

3. Accessibility Service API Usage

This app uses the Accessibility Service API to detect when the power menu is opened and to override it with a custom fake power menu.

  • The API is used solely for this purpose to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • The app does not change user settings without permission.
  • The app does not work around Android built-in privacy controls and notifications, or change the user interface in a deceptive manner.
  • The app does not use the Accessibility Service API for remote call audio recording.

4. Internet Usage

The Fake Power Off application does not use the internet for any purpose. All functionalities of the app are performed strictly locally on your device.

5. Child Privacy

The application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not collect, store, or share any information about children through our app.

6. Security & Conclusion

Although the Fake Power Off application does not collect or transmit any data, we are committed to ensuring that any features within the app are secure and cannot be exploited to compromise your device.

By using the Fake Power Off application, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Your privacy and data security are of utmost importance to us, and we strive to ensure that our app reflects this commitment.
